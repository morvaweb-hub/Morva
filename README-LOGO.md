# Add your logo

Save the gold MORVA logo image (the one you sent in chat) as:

    public/logo.png

Recommended:
- Square, at least 256×256, transparent background (PNG or WebP).
- If you want crisper rendering at all sizes, also save `public/logo@2x.png` at 512×512 — Next/Image will pick the right one automatically.

Until you add the file, the navbar will show a broken-image icon. Drop your `logo.png` in this folder and refresh the page.
